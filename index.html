About

I recently defended my PhD on generating and evaluating synthetic clinical data under privacy constraints. My work studies the privacy–utility trade-off in medical text: producing facsimile documents with large language models (DP fine-tuning, iterative DPO), measuring re-identification risk through linkage and proximity attacks, and validating utility on downstream tasks such as clinical entity extraction and ICD coding.
